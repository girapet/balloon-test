Balloon Test Introduction

Go to

A resident on Route 123 near Old Forest Road wants to erect a cell phone tower on their property. Due to citizen concerns, the town Zoning Board has ordered them to do a balloon test at two different heights to let residents and homeowners know if they will be able to see it.

This web app has a survey that collects your balloon observations. It sends these to a common database and provides a map to see the results.

The survey will work on your phone even when it's not connected to the internet. Observations you collect will be stored until an internet connection becomes available. They are preserved even if you close your phone's web browser.

The survey will ask for permission to use your phone's location and camera. The location is required for the survey to work.
